Welcome

The City is Yours

A new mayor is cleaning up the city—fast. Patrols are shifting, pressure is rising, and a major takedown is coming.

They can't be everywhere at once. Sometimes a zone runs hot. Other times, it's wide open.

Pick your spots, split your resources, and stay off the radar. Build your operation. Read the streets. When it's over, only one crew runs the town.

Setup

Getting Started

  • 3–6 players, 4 rounds
  • Each player starts with 1 resource
  • Three zones: The Docks, The Strip, The Slums
  • Most resources after 4 rounds wins. Tiebreak: least heat
Each Round

The Allocation

  • Choose exactly 2 zones — your crew goes to each
  • Split all your resources between them however you want
  • No resources? Your crew still goes — both zones get 1 crew each
  • Everyone's allocations are revealed at the same time
Zone Resolve

Reading the Heat

  • Highest — Hot zone. Cops swarm it. Lose everything you sent.
  • Middle — Even. Keep what you sent and gain +1 resource.
  • Lowest — Clean run. Triple everything you sent there.
Ties

Split Attention

  • Tied lowest — Light attention. Double that allocation. The remaining highest zone is swarmed and loses everything.
  • Tied highest — Split attention. Halve that allocation (round down). The remaining lowest zone triples.
  • All three tied — Citywide crackdown. For each zone where you sent resources, lose 1 resource.
Heat

Don't Get Burned

  • From Round 2 onward, begin a round with 0 resources? You gain heat.
  • At game end: most resources wins
  • Still tied? Least heat wins. Still tied → draw.
  • Always keep at least 1 resource to stay cool

Return to the main menu?

End the game and return everyone to the lobby? Scores and round progress will reset.
